# CEO Operating Patterns

## Strategic Delegation

- Always activate both Product and Engineering lanes simultaneously
- Assign guardrail tasks to meta-roles to maintain organizational health
- Use numbered plan artifacts (CEO-DEL-XXX) for tracking delegation cycles
- Reference plan artifacts in all delegated task descriptions

## Organization Design

- Two-lane model: Product (PM) and Engineering (FE) as primary execution paths
- Meta-roles as organizational guardrails (Risk, Context, Metrics, Org Effectiveness)
- Direct reports include both lane leads and meta-role owners
- Self-propelling organization with minimal CEO intervention

## Decision Making

- Delegate to lane leads for execution autonomy
- Use meta-roles for risk mitigation and organizational learning
- Focus on strategic exceptions and conflict resolution
- Maintain KPIs: velocity <4h, handoff <30m, 90%+ quality rate

## Monitoring Approach

- Track delegation effectiveness through meta-role reports
- Baseline metrics from initial task cycle
- Pattern extraction for continuous improvement
